About Me

I'm a control system engineer based in Lienz (Osttirol) and Villach (Kärnten), Austria, working as a freelancer on projects that bridge hardware and software. My background covers desktop and embedded application development with Qt: C++/QML, EV charging infrastructure built on the Open Charge Point Protocol (OCPP) and industrial automation using PLCs. I work with clients across Austria and the DACH region.
